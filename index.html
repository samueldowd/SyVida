Initiatives

The following are specific initiatives that SyV has undertaken in El Salvador.
Technology
SyV works with community leadership to improve and maintain technology resources.
Immersion
SyV supports Sacred Heart University's* annual immersive mission trip to El Salvador.
Occupational Therapy
SyV works side by side with community medical workers to assist with OT services and consults.
Community
SyV's most important initiative is learning about and becoming part of the community of El Salvador.

Mission Statement

Solidaridad y Vida stands in solidarity with our brothers and sisters in El Salvador to walk the journey of life. Solidaridad y Vida recognizes that the plight of the Salvadoran people is not our responsibility to solve. By respecting their dignity and showing our dedication, we commit ourselves to learning, experiencing, and supporting, each person we meet, in their own journey.

SyV Blog

SyV and Tierra Blanca, Usulutan in El Salvador

"No son proyectos. Es la vida."

Through immersive experiences of living in, and with, the community of Tierra Blanca in the Salvadoran province of Usulutan, SyV's primary responsibility is to build lasting and meaningful relationships with the members of the community. SyV's mission, as stated in our mission statement, is not to solve problems or serve the poor. SyV's mission is Solidarity and Life.

SyV is not an organization that creates projects at home, and flies to El Salvador to implement their plan and complete specific objectives. We recognize that we are becoming part of the lives of the community members we meet. As the community says about the work they do, "These are not projects. It's life."
